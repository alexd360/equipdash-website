-   Account Data: Retained for the duration of the subscription plus 30

-   Customer Data and End-User Data: Retained during the subscription

-   Website Usage Data: Retained for up to 26 months (consistent with
    standard analytics retention).

-   Legal Obligations: We may retain certain data longer if required by
    law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., resolving disputes,
    enforcing agreements).

6\. DATA SECURITY

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational
measures to protect your information.

-   Encryption: Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS
    1.2+).

-   Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for
    staff, least-privilege principles.

-   Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS with SOC 2 certified infrastructure.

-   Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for unauthorized access and
    security threats.

-   Incident Response: We maintain incident response procedures and will
    notify affected parties of security breaches as required by law.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or
storage is 100% secure.
